Who we are

We are a dedicated medical courier team focused on safe, traceable, and time-critical deliveries. From small clinics to large hospital networks, we operate as an extension of your staff.

Specialized in healthcare logistics

Our routes and procedures are designed specifically for medical transport: lab specimens, pathology, pharmacy items, and medical supplies.

Drivers are trained to follow strict protocols for handling, chain-of-custody, and privacy, ensuring that your operations run smoothly and your patients are protected.
