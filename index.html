About us

Who we are

BioExpressFL is a medical courier focused on safe, traceable, and time-critical deliveries. From small clinics to hospital networks, we operate as an extension of your staff.

Specialized in healthcare logistics

Our procedures are built specifically for medical transport: lab specimens, pathology, pharmacy items, and medical supplies.

Drivers are trained to follow strict protocols for handling, chain-of-custody, and privacy, supporting the reliability your team needs every day.
