About BioExpressFL

What we do

BioExpressFL manages the daily logistics behind your medical deliveries, so your team can stay focused on what matters most: delivering accurate care to patients, on time.

We coordinate the transport of lab specimens, pharmaceuticals, and critical medical supplies between facilities, with routes and timing aligned to the realities of labs, clinics, and hospitals – not generic logistics windows.

Our drivers are trained to respect privacy, follow clear procedures, and treat every order as a critical part of the care journey – never just another package to move from point A to point B.

Our operations are built around:
  • Exposure-control procedures and careful handling of sensitive materials.
  • HIPAA and PHI-aware workflows for specimens, documentation, and records.
  • Driver training focused on chain-of-custody and proof-of-delivery.
  • Clear packaging and labeling guidelines when working with carriers and partners.

A letter from our founder

Why BioExpressFL exists

Over the years working alongside healthcare teams, I saw how much time and energy was lost trying to manage deliveries that should simply “just work”. Late specimens, missed cut-off times, and unclear status updates don’t just create stress — they delay care.

BioExpressFL was built to remove that friction. Our goal is to give your staff reliable, medical-only transportation that feels like an extension of your own team. From the moment an order is placed to the moment it’s delivered, we want you to feel confident that everything is moving on schedule.

We use modern tools for routing and communication so your team can stay informed without extra steps. At the same time, we take the time to understand how your lab, clinic, or hospital actually works, and adapt our service to your routines, not the other way around.

Every client we serve is treated like a long-term partner. We’re here to help you protect patient privacy, meet time-sensitive commitments, and keep your operations flowing smoothly — one delivery at a time.

L. Cardoso
Founder · BioExpressFL Medical Courier
