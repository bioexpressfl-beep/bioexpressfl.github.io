About BioExpressFL

What we do

BioExpressFL manages the daily logistics behind your medical deliveries, so your team can stay focused on what matters most: delivering accurate care to patients, on time.

We handle the movement of lab specimens, pharmaceuticals, and essential medical supplies between facilities, coordinating routes and timing around the real-world demands of labs, clinics, and hospitals.

Our drivers are trained to respect privacy, follow clear procedures, and treat every order as a critical piece of the care journey – not just another box to move from point A to B.

Our operations are built around:
  • Exposure-control procedures and safe handling of sensitive materials.
  • HIPAA and PHI-aware workflows for specimens and documentation.
  • Driver training with focus on chain-of-custody and proof-of-delivery.
  • Clear guidelines for packaging, labeling, and working with commercial carriers.
